Chapter 1
Introduction

1.1 What?

  • A GPL 3D computer graphics software for creating animated ﬁlms, visual eﬀects, art, 3D (printed) models, 3D applications and video games.

1.2 Who and when?

  • Ton Roosendaal, the main contributor of Blender, founded NaN (Not a Number) Technologies in June 1998 and start sharing Blender as shareware.
  • NaN went backrupt in 2002, and together with Neo Geo, open-source Blender using 100,000 Euros collected from the community.
